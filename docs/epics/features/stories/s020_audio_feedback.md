# Story: Audio Feedback

**Feature:** [Enhanced Camera Feedback](../f007_enhanced_camera_feedback.md)

**Description:** Add audio effects that provide feedback when photos are taken and clues are captured, enhancing the immersive experience.

**Requirements:** Implement sound effects that respond to player actions and successful/unsuccessful clue captures.

**Constraints:** Audio should match the game's atmosphere and not be jarring or repetitive.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
*   [ ] Camera shutter sound when taking photos
*   [ ] Distinct success sound when capturing a clue
*   [ ] Failure or neutral sound for non-clue photos
*   [ ] Special sound effects for sequence completion or rare clues
