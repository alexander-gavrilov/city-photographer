# City Photographer

## Purpose

The City Photographer is a photography application designed to capture and showcase stunning images of urban landscapes. Whether you are a professional photographer or an enthusiast, this application provides an easy-to-use interface to manage your photography projects.

## Major Features

- **User-Friendly Interface**: Intuitive design that makes it easy to upload and manage photos.
- **Project Organization**: Organize your photos into different projects for better management.
- **Photo Editing Tools**: Basic editing tools to enhance your images before showcasing them.
- **Social Media Integration**: Share your projects directly on social media platforms.
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on both desktop and mobile devices.

## Setup Instructions

1. **Clone the Repository**:  
   `git clone https://github.com/alexander-gavrilov/city-photographer.git`
2. **Navigate to the Directory**:  
   `cd city-photographer`
3. **Install Dependencies**:  
   Follow the setup instructions found in the `INSTALL.md` file.
4. **Run the Application**:  
   Launch the application using your preferred method.

## Usage

- **Uploading Photos**: Use the upload button on the main page to add photos to your projects.
- **Editing Photos**: Select a photo and use the editing tools to adjust brightness, contrast, and more.
- **Organizing Projects**: Group related photos under a single project to keep your portfolio organized.

Enjoy capturing the beauty of your city with City Photographer!
